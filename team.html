Dr. Abdulrahman Alhamed

Dr. Alhamed obtained a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering with distinction from King Saud University in 2016, followed by M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California San Diego in 2019 and 2021, correspondingly. He has held positions at Électricité de France and Nokia Bell Labs, where he focused on the development of cutting-edge wireless communication circuit design. Dr. Alhamed served as a Senior Member of the Technical Staff within the Silicon Engineering Group at Apple Inc between 2021 and 2022.
In 2023, Dr. Alhamed joined the College of Engineering at King Saud University, where he currently holds the position of Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering. His research interests encompass the intersection of sensing and communication systems, with a particular emphasis on integrated phased-array systems, radars, and imaging sensors. Dr. Alhamed was the recipient of the 2021 Analog Devices Outstanding Designer Award.
